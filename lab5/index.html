Artificial Intelligence

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are designed to think and learn. AI systems can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as problem-solving, speech recognition, and decision-making.

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly transforming the world. While it offers immense benefits, it is crucial to address its challenges to ensure responsible development and usage.

Types of Artificial Intelligence

  • Narrow AI: Specialized in one task, such as voice assistants like Siri or Alexa.
  • General AI: Possesses human-like intelligence across a range of activities.
  • Super AI: Theoretical AI that surpasses human intelligence.
